Monte Carlo Method: Sum, Integration and Matrix Multiplication

Li-Ke Ma

by Li-Ke Ma, 2018-2-3

There are plenty of articles mention about Monte Carlo algorithm, however non of them investigate deeper in its techniques. This article is a note from Chapter 31 of Computer Graphics Principle and Practice, which may show some practical techniques of Monte Carlo algorithm.

Monte Carlo algorithm is mainly used in situation where directly integration is hard or impossible. By sampling following a specific distribution and averaging those samplings, we can get a good estimation of the target integration.
